Understanding Your Dosha

Vata Dominant Body Type

With dominance of air and space element, you are a creative, liberal, and fun-loving person. Your hair, skin, nails, and other body parts tend to be dry, cool, light, and rough. You may have a slim and light body.

Diet for Vata

To balance the dry, cool, and light effect of vata dosha, consume more oily, warm, and heavy food according to your digestive strength. Herbs like garlic, ginger, clove, and black cardamom are great for you.

Lifestyle for Vata

Try to have a regular routine, especially meal times. Mindfulness practice and chanting may help balance mental hyperactivity and maintain a focused approach.

Pitta Dominant Body Type

Fire is the dominant element in Pitta body type. You are intelligent, classy, and vigilant with a practical approach towards life. You have great metabolism, good appetite, and an athletic personality.

Diet for Pitta

You must have cooling and moisturising herbs to balance the fire element. Cooling herbs like coriander, green cardamom, fennel, and vetiver are great for you.

Lifestyle for Pitta

Avoid skipping meals. Afternoon nap on a hot day is good for you. Regular meditation may help balance anger and frustration and develop unconditional acceptance.

Kapha Dominant Body Type

With abundance of water and earth elements, you are a forgiving, peaceful, and steady person with excellent memory. You may have beautiful skin, thick hair, and a heavy build.

Diet for Kapha

Balance excess moisture, coolness, and heaviness with dry, light, and warm herbs like turmeric, ginger, asafoetida, and caraway.

Lifestyle for Kapha

Avoid over-eating and sleeping during daytime. Exercise and active routine is a must to avoid weight gain. Music meditation can help knock out inactivity and dullness.
